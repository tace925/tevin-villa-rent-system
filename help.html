Help & Support

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new tenant?
Go to the Tenants page and click the "Add New Tenant" button. Fill in the required information and click Save.
How do I record a payment?
Navigate to the Payments page and click "Record Payment". Select the tenant, enter the amount, and choose the payment method.
Is my data secure?
Yes! All data is stored securely in Firebase with proper authentication and security rules.
Can I access the system on mobile?
Absolutely! Tevin Villa is fully responsive and works on all devices - phones, tablets, and computers.
